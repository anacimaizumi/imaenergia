Engineering company

Created in August of 2020 and located in Londrina/Paraná Brazil IMA Energia is a company which provides services in Engineering to meet demands on Electrical Machines, specially generators, for end customers, manufacturers, repair shops, material suppliers or suppliers of electrical equipment

IMA Energia has a highly qualified technical team with more than 10 years of experience in the market, operating in a wide variety of machines whose rated data vary from 11,2 to 679 MVA 6,9 to 21 kV and 81,82 to 900 rpm

Solutions

Preparation of technical specification of new machines and machines in refurbishment, rehabilitation or uprating

Electromagnetic and insulation design of new machines and machines in refurbishment, rehabilitation or uprating

Preparation of Assembly, O&M and Commissioning manuals

Analysis of electrical machine design documents

Root cause analysis of failures

Workshop and Field inspections and electrical tests

Development of new materials and qualification of manufacturing processes

Technical training
